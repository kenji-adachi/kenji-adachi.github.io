Project: Pipesim

Senior Project

Pipesim is the senior design project for my university. Me and two other team members work with a client to complete the requirements of the client. We are working on updating and adding features to an existing software for the client to use in research. The premise of the project is working with Python to make a pipe water flow simulation. We hold weekly or bi-weekly meetings with the client and hold team meetings to cover any current updates to the project or requirements. Currently the project is still in progress and most information must remain disclosed until the research paper is published.
